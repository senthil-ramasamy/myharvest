How to get started ?

It’s all about your first step towards a happier and healthier living. You have the interest & the space to grow but not sure of where to begin? Here is what we at “myHarvest” do !

You choose a gardening package

We offer various kitchen gardening packages that enables your family to grow greens, vegetables, herbs and fruits. We also customize the garden as per your family needs. View Packages

We Setup the garden

On the scheduled date, our garden expert team brings all gardening materials including the soil, set up the kitchen garden and guides you to become an urban farmer.

YOU GROW and harvest

Every day you water the plants and observe them closely. Within a month, you can start harvesting and eating home grown food.

WE HELP YOU MAINTAIN

Planting a kitchen garden is a lot like raising children. Plants need your care & minimal time. Having realized this, we are there to make sure your plants are healthy all the time.
